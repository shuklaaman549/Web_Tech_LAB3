Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Tomato

Instructions

1. Cook the bacon in a pan until crispy.
2. Slice the tomato.
3. Place a slice of cheese between two slices of bread.
4. Place the sandwich in a pan and cook on both sides until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
5. Add the cooked bacon and tomato slices to the sandwich.
6. Serve and enjoy!
